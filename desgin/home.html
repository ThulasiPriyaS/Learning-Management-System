Introduction to Learning Management System

Welcome to the world of digital learning! A Learning Management System (LMS) is a comprehensive software application that allows the management and delivery of educational content. It facilitates a dynamic learning environment by providing a platform for interactive discussions, assessments, and the distribution of educational materials. With the integration of modern tools and technological advancements, the LMS offers a seamless solution for both learners and educators.
